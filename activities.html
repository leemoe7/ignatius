CURRICULUM ACTIVITIES

Education For Life

Ignatius pre -primary school is a day Jesuit school which strives to develop men and women of competence, conciensience and compassion. It is collaborative process between and among faculty and students that fosters personal and cooperative study, discovery, creativity and reflection to promote lifelong learning and action in service to others.

Education for life reminds us what we teach to the pupils should not end in class and also shouldn’t it be only about imparting knowledge to kids but rather it should contain a total package of exploring abilities, capabilities, interests and behaviors of our pupils and make the most of them in molding the pupils mindset in developing interest with school and be able to transform the way pupils think and adopting in the ways of living. Education for life prepares the child to face the challenges of living as a human being and helps him to achieve balance and harmony in all he engages himself.

Academic performance is one of the core activities within a school. We prepare pupils for the all types of evaluation such as standard four national examinations and standard seven national examinations. We are proud of our major achievements since the school began in 2003; we have always been outstanding as we rank among top ten schools in the region. This proves the quality expertise produced in our school.

Ignatius is well equipped to meet all the modern requirement of the quality education e.g. enough professional teachers, teaching and learning materials. A system of continuous assessment, test, visiting day and parents meetings, allows staff and parent to keep closer eye on each pupil’s academic progress.

By Mr. Lewis Mshanga

A.M.D.G
